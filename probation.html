Probation Week

Welcome to our probation week, the final step before commencing your course journey. Upon successful completion, you'll transition into the boot camp, where real learning begins.

During this period, we'll evaluate your performance and dedication, as it plays a crucial role in shaping your future in the program. Best of luck on this important stage of your educational journey!"

  • Keep in mind once you complete this then you go to bootcamp

    • Details

    Probation period duration

    • One week

    Performance expectation and evaluation criteria

    • You must attend all the sessions
    • You must complete given assignments
    • You must show your seriousness throughout the week in sessions, assignments, and discussions

    Mentorship or training opportunities during probation

    • The industry leaders give you mentorship regarding your technical and non-technical skills
    • They tell you how the industry actually works and how to train yourself for the industry
    • They share their best practices and techniques about interviews, jobs, startups, and how you present yourself to someone
