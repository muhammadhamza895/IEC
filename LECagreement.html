The student has to contribute IEC 10% of their monthly salary for 24 months starting from the employment date. This will help sponsor another student like yourself in need of acquiring new skills and jobs.

Upon fulfilling the above three requirements, the student will become a valued member of the IEC Community. You will have access to networking opportunities, alumni events, and continued support from our organization.
