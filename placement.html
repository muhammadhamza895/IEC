Placements

Job Placement

Job placement means finding your dream job after training. We offer valuable assistance and resources to connect you with potential employers for better opportunities.

Freelancing

In the Bootcamp, you'll master freelancing - working independently on diverse projects for clients. Learn to find clients, negotiate contracts, and deliver exceptional work for success.

Job Hunting

Gain confidence in the job market with our essential skills training - finding opportunities, crafting impressive resumes and cover letters, and excelling in interviews.

What you'll learn

In this section, you will learn the following essential skills in the order mentioned

1

Understanding the Job Market: Gain insights into the job market, understanding current trends and demands.

2

Identifying Job Opportunities: Learn effective techniques to find job openings that align with your career goals.

3

Networking for Job Search: Discover the power of networking to expand your connections and job opportunities.

4

Freelancing Essentials: Understand the fundamentals of freelancing, how to find freelance projects, and manage client relationships.

5

Building an Online Portfolio: Create an impressive online portfolio to showcase your work and skills.

6

Negotiation Skills: Learn negotiation tactics to secure the best job offers and freelance contracts.

7

Job Application Etiquette: Understand proper job application etiquette to leave a positive impression on employers.
